FAQs

How do I change a tee time?

You cannot cancel a round, but you may request a different round (of equal or lesser value) by filling out the form. Unless this is related to bad weather, round changes must be made at least 1 hour before the round begins.

How much does it cost to golf?

Most rounds cost between $8 and $20, depending on the course and what is included. All rounds include cart; some rounds include driving range (prior to the round) or breakfast.
about divinebacknine
DivineBackNine was created so guys could golf on a regular basis throughout the year without breaking the bank or burdening schedules. DivineBackNine is a self-supporting outreach several churches and businesses.
