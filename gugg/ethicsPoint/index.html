Guggenheim Partners upholds its obligation to employees, clients, vendors and partners to maintain the highest ethical standards while conducting business. As exemplified in our Guiding Principles, we strive to maintain integrity and achieve excellence in all that we do.

Guggenheim Partners promotes a culture of integrity with an environment of open, honest communication. We want you to feel comfortable in approaching your supervisor or management in instances where you believe violations of policies or standards have occurred.

Guggenheim Partners values and respects diversity and inclusion and is committed to a work environment in which all individuals are treated with respect and dignity. For information about harassment, see Guggenheim's Non-Discrimination and Harassment Prevention Policy (which is available to all employees on our OneGuggenheim intranet page).

In situations where you do not prefer to approach your supervisor, our Code of Conduct (which is available to all employees on our OneGuggenheim intranet page) provides other options to report your concern, including this independent website where you may place an anonymous report in confidence. Additionally, you are encouraged to submit reports relating to violations stated in our Code of Conduct.

Depending on your preference, the information that you provide will be sent to us by EthicsPoint on a confidential and anonymous basis. Please know that your comments and concerns will be heard and that Guggenheim Partners prohibits retaliation against those who report a violation or possible violation of law or policy.

It is a violation of Guggenheim's policies to willfully report false information. See the EthicsPoint FAQ's for more information.

EthicsPoint is NOT a 911 or Emergency Service: Do not use this site to report events presenting an immediate threat to life or property. Reports submitted through this service may not receive an immediate response. If you require emergency assistance, please contact your local authorities.
