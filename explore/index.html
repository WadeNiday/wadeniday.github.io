think big

A solid design process provides a framework that is consistent enough to advance large-scale projects, yet flexible enough to improve upon the smallest detail. Maintaining a process helps keep the project focused. It increases workflow efficiency and offers a feedback loop for collaborative improvements.

  • On every project:
  • Content is king, type is queen
  • Clarity is the goal
  • Critique is not attack
  • People matter
  • Understand the problem →

  • A clearly defined problem and
    a firm understanding into any research is critical in setting the
    tone for the project.

  • Sketch lo-fi ideas →

  • Iʼs not pretty, but getting ideas
    on to paper is a way to accelerate
    thinking. For me itʼs both writing
    and sketching.

  • Prototype hi-fi concepts →

  • This is where ideas sink or swim.
    Visual details begin to bring the system to life, yet the look and feel keeps evolving.

  • Design and refine →

  • The prototype and feedback
    loop are now leading the charge towards simplicity, refinement,
    and simplicity. Keep refining.
